Walking in Faith

John 3:16

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."

In this powerful verse, we are reminded of the depth of God's love for us. It's a love that sacrifices, that gives without expecting return, that reaches out to the unworthy and makes them worthy through Christ.

Today, let this truth sink deep into your heart. You are loved with an everlasting love, a love that sent Jesus to the cross for your redemption. This love is not based on your performance but on God's character. Walk in this assurance today.

Pastor Michael Johnson
Senior Pastor
