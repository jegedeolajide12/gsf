Frequently Asked Questions

Is online giving secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and financial information. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.

Can I get a receipt for my donation?

Yes, all donations receive an automatic email receipt. For bank transfers, please email finance@gsffuta.org with your transfer details to request a receipt.

How do I update my recurring donation?

Please contact our finance team at finance@gsffuta.org or call +234 812 345 6789 to update your recurring donation settings.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, GSFFUTA is a registered non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible, and we provide annual contribution statements for tax purposes.
