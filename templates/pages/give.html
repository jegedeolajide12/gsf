Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about donating

How do I get a receipt for my donation?

We automatically send a donation receipt to the email address provided at the time of donation. For bank transfers, please email finance@hopefoundation.org with your transaction details, and we'll send you a receipt within 3-5 business days.

Can I set up recurring donations?

Yes! We offer monthly recurring donation options through our online portal. You can choose the amount, frequency, and which fund you'd like to support. You can modify or cancel your recurring donation at any time.

How much of my donation goes directly to programs?

We're proud that 89% of every dollar donated goes directly to program activities. Only 8% is used for administrative costs and 3% for fundraising expenses. We maintain the highest standards of financial accountability and transparency.

Can I donate securities or stocks?

Yes, we accept donations of stocks and securities. This can be a tax-efficient way to give. Please contact our finance department at investments@hopefoundation.org for transfer instructions and to notify us of your incoming donation.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Hope Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Donations from U.S. taxpayers are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Please consult with your tax advisor regarding specific deductibility.
