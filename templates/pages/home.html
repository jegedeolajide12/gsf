The Path of Grace

"For it is by grace you have been saved, through faithâ€”and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God." - Ephesians 2:8

Grace is the unmerited favor of God, a gift we receive not because of what we've done, but because of who God is. In a world that often measures worth by achievement, grace stands as a radical alternative.

Today, consider how you might extend grace to yourself and others. Remember that your value isn't tied to your productivity or perfection, but to the unconditional love of the Divine.

As you move through your day, look for opportunities to offer kindness without expectation, forgiveness without condition, and love without reservation. This is the path of grace.

First-Time Visitors

We're excited to welcome you to our church family

Welcome to Gofamint Students' Fellowship

We're thrilled you're considering joining us for worship. At Gofamint Students' Fellowship, we strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone can experience God's love and grace.

Whether you're new to faith, returning to church, or just visiting the area, we want your experience with us to be uplifting and meaningful.

  • Our services last about 75 minutes and include worship, prayer, and teaching
  • Casual dress is perfectly acceptable - come as you are!
  • Free parking is available in our main lot and street parking
  • Safe and engaging programs available for children of all ages
  • Accessible facilities for those with mobility challenges

We'd love to connect with you personally after the service. Please stop by our Gen-Sec stand in the lobby to receive a special gift and meet our team.
