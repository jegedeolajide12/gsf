Our History

A journey of faith, growth, and God's faithfulness

GSFFUTA began as a small gathering of Christian students in 1985, meeting under a tree on the FUTA campus. Today, we've grown into a vibrant community of over 500 members, impacting lives for Christ through fellowship, discipleship, and outreach.

1985

The Beginning

A small group of 12 students began meeting for prayer and Bible study under a mango tree on campus, led by Brother Samuel Adeyemi.

1992

Official Recognition

The fellowship was officially recognized by the university as the Gospel Students' Fellowship (GSF) with allocated space in the Chapel of Peace.

2001

First Building Project

Construction began on our first permanent structure - a multi-purpose hall that could accommodate 300 worshippers.

2010

Expansion to Campuses

GSF expanded to include satellite fellowships at FUTA's satellite campuses and affiliate institutions.

2018

Current Facility Dedication

Our new 800-seat auditorium was dedicated, featuring modern facilities for worship and discipleship.
